# BigBMeetup Website, Branding, and Business Strategy Document

### 1. Overview

**BigBMeetup** is more than an initiative ‚Äî it's a **movement**. Founded in April 2018 by **Bosco Menezes** in Qatar under the vision of **BigB Fotografi**, BigBMeetup has spent over seven years bringing people and organizations together through inclusive, creative, and socially meaningful programs. It unites schools, museums, corporates, and local communities around shared values and real impact.

Its five key pillars form the foundation of this community movement:

* **Sports & Wellness**

* **Celebrating Art & Culture**

* **Estedama (Sustainability)**

* **This Ability** ‚Äì Championing people who are differently-abled and neurodiverse

* **Animal Welfare**

Since its inception, BigBMeetup has successfully hosted **13 major editions** ‚Äî each themed around one of these pillars and held at iconic venues such as **The Torch, Aspire Zone**, **Fraser Suites**, **Sheikh Faisal Bin Qasim Al Thani Museum**, and **Qatar Foundation's Multaqa**. These events have highlighted wellness, art, inclusion, sustainability, and social impact through inspiring storytelling, photography, and community engagement.

### 5. Website Plan ‚Äî **Single-Page Strategy**

#### Why a Single Page

* **Simple and powerful** ‚Äî tells the story as one cohesive narrative.

* **Perfect for movements** ‚Äî visitors experience the mission instead of browsing pages.

* **Easy to maintain** ‚Äî only case studies and media get dedicated pages.

* **SEO-friendly** ‚Äî additional pages (stories, YouTube videos, etc.) can still drive organic reach.

#### Single-Page Flow

**1. Hero Section**

* Tagline: *"To Inspire, To Educate, To Inform & Bring Communities Together"*

* Subtext: "A movement that celebrates people, purpose, and positive change in Qatar."

* Visual: looping video montage featuring smiling faces, event snippets, and Qatar landmarks.

* CTAs: [Join the Movement] [Watch Our Story]

**2. About the Movement**

* Narrative of BigBMeetup's journey and highlights from all 13 editions.

* Stats section: "7+ Years | 13 Editions | 50+ Partners | 25,000+ Participants."

**3. The Five Pillars**

* Interactive icon-based section: each pillar reveals its story on hover/click.

* Each links to a themed case-study or video page.

**4. Stories of Impact**

* Embedded YouTube playlist or carousel of key highlights from meetups.

* "See All Stories" ‚Üí media gallery page.

**5. Partners & Collaborators**

* Grid of logos featuring key sponsors, schools, museums, and media outlets.

* Optional CTA: "Become a Partner."

**6. Join the Movement**

* Volunteer and sponsorship inquiry form.

* Newsletter signup to stay connected.

**7. Footer**

* Contact, social media links, and hashtags (#BigBMovement #BringingCommunitiesTogether).
